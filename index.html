Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Kivix.

Does it work without internet?

Yes. LAN mode works offline inside labs, homes, classrooms, and offices. Your devices sync directly over local network.

Does it support images?

Closed beta supports text only. Image sync is coming soon in future updates.

Is Android required?

No. Windows ↔ Windows and Linux ↔ Windows also work perfectly. Mix and match any platforms you need.

Will it replace Phone Link?

Kivix works across ALL platforms, not just Windows + Android. It's truly universal and cross-platform.

Is it free?

Closed beta is 100% free for all testers. Future pricing will be announced after beta completion.

Can I use it in college labs?

Yes! LAN mode works perfectly in local networks. Ideal for educational institutions and collaborative environments.
